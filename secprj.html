Project Description

Logo is an E-Commerce website where they sell sport equipments
The project focused on securing OWASP Top 10 vulnerabilities.
OWASP 10:

  • A01:2021 - Broken Access Control
  • A03:2021 - Injection
  • A07:2021 - Indentification and Authentication Failures

My areas of contribution:

  • Account lockout with incremental timeout after consecutive failed login attempts
  • Two factor authentication (2FA) via Google Authenticator app
  • Alerting App Incident via Email
  • Rate limiting the application to prevent DoS attack and limit web scraping
